Histogram of Latitudes


The motivation behind this project was to use data to answer the question: "What's the weather like as we approach the equator?"

We started by drawing a random sample of cities across the latitude spectrum. The data was extracted on 13 January 2020 using the OpenWeatherMap API.

The histogram shows that the sample of randomly selected cities is skewed towards the north hemisphere and concentrated between the tropic of cancer (23° 26′ North) and the Arctic Circle (66° 34′ North).
